Merriton Landing

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Top Hotels

Hotel Taniti

Hotel Taniti is a four-star hotel located in the heart of Taniti. It is a short walk from the beach and has a pool, a gym, and a restaurant. The hotel has 100 rooms and is a popular choice for tourists.

Sunset Bay Resort

Sunset Bay Resort is a luxurious five-star hotel overlooking the pristine beaches of Taniti. Guests can enjoy oceanfront suites, an infinity pool, a full-service spa, and multiple fine dining options. With 150 rooms, it's perfect for those seeking a high-end retreat.

Palm Grove Inn

Palm Grove Inn is a charming three-star hotel nestled in a quiet corner of Taniti. It offers cozy accommodations, a lush garden courtyard, and a casual caf√©. With 50 rooms, it's an excellent choice for budget-conscious travelers looking for comfort and convenience.
