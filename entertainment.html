Merriton Landing

Merriton landing is a rapidly developing area on the northern side of Yellow Leaf Bay. If you wish to entertain yourself with various activities such as golfing, playing at an arcade, watching movies, dancing at the club, or bowling, consider visiting this area!

Popular Activities

There are plenty of other ways to spend your time on the island. While relaxing at the beach, exploring the rainforest, and climbing the volcano are some of the more popular ones, you can also do things such as:

  • Visiting a local history museum
  • Visit art galleries
  • Going on chartered fishing tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Rainforest zip-lining
  • Visiting pubs (including a microbrewery!)
  • Taking a helicopter ride
